Hunza Valley

Hunza known as Heaven on Earth, located in Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan. Situated at north/west of Hunza River, surrounded by several high peaks including Rakaposhi, Hunza Peak, BojahagurDuanasir II, Darmyani Peak, Ghenta Sar, Ultar Sar and Ladyfinger Peak.

Much renowned Hunza valley is often referred to as heaven on earth, enveloped in the grand Himalayas and the Karakoram mountain ranges, this place has been a great tourist attraction for many years.

Hunza appears as a jewl among beautiful and serene valleys of Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan.From winters to spring, the nature seems to be in a transition period.The enchanting sights of nature make worth watching views.

Hunza is probably Pakistan's most visited valley, by the tourists. It is a fairy tale land surrounded by beautiful rugged & snow capped mountains. Only at a distance of 100 Kms. from Gilgit Hunza is a small town on Karakorum Highway. At the altitude of 7000 - 800 feet it is the first main town or stop.It is a spectacular journey through some of the most interesting villages of Karakorum Highway
